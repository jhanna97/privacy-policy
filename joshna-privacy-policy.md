# Joshnahanna's Event Planning Privacy Policy

## Overview

This is a personal event planning application developed by Joshnahanna that integrates with Pinterest to display board inspiration for hens parties and events.

## Data Collection

- We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data
- The application only retrieves publicly available Pinterest board data that you authorize access to
- No cookies, tracking, or analytics are implemented
- All data access is controlled solely by you

## Data Usage

- Pinterest board data is displayed for personal event planning purposes only
- No data is shared with third parties
- All data remains under your control
- We do not store or retain any Pinterest board information

## Your Rights

- You can revoke access at any time through your Pinterest account settings
- You have full control over which boards are shared with this application
- No data persists after you revoke access

Joshnahanna may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be posted to this page.
